# Buffer Status Report plots

The upper panel is the backlog reported to the AP and the lower panel is the
backlog selected by the UL scheduler. Closely tracking curves indicate fresh
BSR information. Delayed steps, scheduling after the reported backlog has
changed, or prolonged plateaus are expected in the stale-BSR scenario.

The signals are AP-side event observations. They are not interpreted as
regularly sampled queue lengths and are therefore drawn as steps.
